The Best Movies According to Maria

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Harry Potter

A magical journey following the story of a young wizard and his friends as they face dark forces in a richly imagined world. The blend of fantasy, adventure, and emotional storytelling makes this series unforgettable.

Prisoners

A gripping psychological thriller that explores the lengths a father will go to save his missing daughter. Featuring intense performances and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, this film is a masterpiece of suspense.

Gone Girl

A dark, intricately crafted mystery that delves into the complexities of marriage and media manipulation. Stunningly directed and filled with twists, this movie is both beautiful and haunting.
